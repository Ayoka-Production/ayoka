Samba Diallo

Choreographer and Founder of Afro Fit by Samba.

I am an International Choreographer, performer, and actor.
I am formerly of the national ballet of the Ivory Coast. I am the founder of Afro-Fit, a dance fitness program that motivate people to stay healthy by dancing.I teach and peform African dance and culture throughout the world.
